Om festivalen

Fælles­skab
under
trækronerne.

Arnakke Festival er en tre dages festival på Arnakke Gaard i Svinninge, med musik, taler, dans, mad og leg! Festivalen startede som en fødselsdagsfest i gårdens baghave i 2020, hvor corona-epidemien havde tvunget de store festivaler i knæ.

Festivalen startede som en fødselsdagsfest i gårdens baghave i 2020, hvor corona-epidemien havde tvunget de store festivaler i knæ. 50 smukke unge mennesker, der kunne slænge sig i hængekøjer i solen og danse sammen under træerne i diskokuglens skær.

Siden er festivalen kun vokset sig større og smukkere. Her bliver traktorer og mejetærskere kørt til side og giver plads til koncerter, talks og langborde fyldt med overdådig lokal mad, så vi i fællesskab kan skabe nogle fortryllende dage sammen.

Vi ønsker at være en festival med fokus på fællesskab og nærhed — hvor vi griber hinanden i musikken og legen, og kommer tæt på hinanden.

I år er det syvende gang at festivalen holdes, og vi synes at DU skal med, uanset om du er ny eller gammel. Så tag dine venner under armen og inviter dem til begivenheden!

Arnakke Festival — July 9–12, 2026

Once again this year, Arnakke Festival welcomes you to three unforgettable summer days for new and old friends alike. We can hardly wait to see you celebrating under the treetops — everyone is welcome!

Tickets

Ticket sales open on March 26 at 6:00 PM. It will be possible to purchase both full festival passes and one-day tickets.

With a full festival pass, you get three days of music, events, workshops, homemade breakfast buns, long-table dinners with greens from the farm, parties for as long as you like, an affordable bar, and new friendships!

More information about ticket prices coming soon.

What is Arnakke Festival?

Arnakke Festival is a three-day festival held at Arnakke Gaard in Svinninge, featuring music, talks, dancing, food, and play! The festival began as a birthday party in the farm's backyard in 2020, when the COVID-19 pandemic had brought the major festivals to a halt. Arnakke Gaard opened its doors to 50 wonderful young people who could lounge in hammocks in the sun and dance together under the trees in the glow of a disco ball.

Tractors and harvesters are moved aside to make room for concerts, talks, and long tables filled with abundant local food. We strive to be a festival focused on community and closeness — where we truly come close to one another!

This year marks the seventh edition of the festival. So grab your friends and invite them to the event!

Practical Info

Arnakke Festival takes place from Wednesday, July 9th to Saturday, July 12th, 2026.

Information about the music program, events, transportation, food, and more will be shared on an ongoing basis.
