Hvad er Arnakke Festival? 🌻

De sidste 6 år har Arnakke Gaard huset festivalen. I en forlænget weekend i sommeren er det lokale landbrug blevet suppleret med opdyrkning af fællesskaber, musikoplevelser og langbordsmiddage.

Igen i år gentager vi succesen, og vi synes at du skal med. Vi har fokus på nærhed, både ift. til de lokale råvarer menuen er bygget op omkring, de mennesker festivalen fyldes af, og at musikoplevelsen kommer helt tæt på. Arnakke bringer nye minder og venner i nærheden af dig.

Og så ses vi i skoven til Arnakke Festival 🌲

ARNAKKE FESTIVAL – 9-12 July 2026 ☀️

Once again this year, Arnakke Festival welcomes you to 3 unforgettable summer days. We look forward to seeing you for a celebration beneath the treetops.

Ticket Sales 🧾

Partout - 575 kr.
One day ticket - 275 kr.

With the all-access ticket, enjoy 3 days of music, events, food, and endless fellowship.

What is Arnakke Festival? 🌻

For the past 6 years, Arnakke Gaard has hosted the festival. During an extended summer weekend, local farming is complemented by the cultivation of communities, musical experiences, and long-table dinners. This year, we're repeating the success—and we believe you should be a part of it.

We emphasize closeness, from the local produce that shapes our menu, to the people who fill the festival, and the intimate musical experience. Arnakke brings new memories and friends close to you.

See you in the forest at Arnakke Festival. 🌲

Practical Info 📋

Arnakke Festival takes place from Wednesday, 9th July to Saturday, 12th July.

The music program, menu, and further information will be announced continuously.

If you have any questions, please contact one of the event's administrators.
